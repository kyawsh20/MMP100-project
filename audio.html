Audio Project

In my audio project, I depicted a person waking up from a rough night, taking a shower, preparing for a trip, and driving to the airport because after a busy time with work and school, I wanted to imagine relaxing and traveling around the world. I used 15 downloaded audio samples, including alarm sounds, driving sounds, walking sounds, plane sounds, and shower sounds. I used fade-outs on sounds like the plane, alarm, driving, and walking, and I also cut and re-edited the audio to make everything flow better. The hardest part was finding the best audio and figuring out how to tell the story clearly.
