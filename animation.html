Animation Project

For my animation, I chose the emotion of stress and expressed it through a crashing effect. My animation changed from the storyboard, where it was originally a rock, to a final version where I decided to use a fireball flying through space instead. I used all four transform properties — position, rotation, scale, and opacity — throughout the animation. The most challenging part of the project was adding text and adjusting scales and movement in After Effects.
